BUFFY AND YUME

By Devraj Chudasama




Who is Buffy and Yume?

What do the names Buffy and Yume respresent. Well actually, they are the very cute dogs of the most powerful person, Vladmir Putin. Putin is currently the prime minister of Russia, and is viewed as a very scary strong indivisual. We can go on and on about Putin, but lets stay focused on the two cute creatures Putin posseses, and shed some background information relating to their breeds.

Buffy

Buffy, is a caramel and white Bulgarian 11 year old male Karakachan breed dog. It is Putin's second dog and it was gifted to him by Bulgarian prime minister Boyko Borisov during Putin's visit to Bulgaria in November 2010.

Karakachan Breed

This dog breed originates from the county of Bulgaria. During historic times in Bulgaria, this dog breed was used as a border army watchdog. In todays times, they are formally used for defending property and livestock.



Yume

Yume is Putins latest dog. Yume is a 9 year old female Akita bred dog. The dog was gifted to him by a regional government within Japan to show gratitude for Russia's help involving a earthquake.

Akita Species

The Akita dog breed originates from the mountainous regions of Japan. This breed of dog resembles characteristics of being powerful, dominant, and independent. They show love to their close companions, but are weary of complete strangers.
